About HUMSS

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand focuses on the study of human behavior, society, culture, politics, and communication. It prepares students for careers in fields that involve working with people, understanding social issues, and developing communication and critical thinking skills.

Skills Developed

Students improve their communication, research, critical thinking, and leadership skills.

Possible Careers

Lawyer, Teacher, Journalist, Psychologist, Politician, Social Worker, and Public Speaker.

Subjects

Creative Writing, Politics and Governance, World Religions, Philippine Culture, and Social Sciences.

Purpose

The HUMSS strand prepares students for college programs related to humanities, education, law, communication, and social sciences.
